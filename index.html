(02)

A designer who writes code, a developer who draws curves.

I've spent the last seven years at the intersection of design and engineering — shipping products, identities and interactive stories with studios like Aurora, Tidewater and a handful of lovely founders.

My work lives in the gap between static and alive. Every easing curve, every rest state, every pixel of reveal is a tiny chance to make the web feel more human.

Currently exploring the overlap of generative motion, type, and agentic interfaces. Slowly, on purpose.
